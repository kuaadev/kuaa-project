Low Powered FM Community Radio Station in Salt Lake City

KUAA 99.9 FM is a low powered FM community radio station broadcasting terrestrially in Salt Lake City at 99.9FM, and streaming around the world at kuaafm.org. We pride ourselves on specializing in musical, cultural, linguistic, and artistic diversity. KUAA is an extension of and brought to you by Utah Arts Alliance. station.
